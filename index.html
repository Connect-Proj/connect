Short Claps

For a student, who is aspiring to crack any campus placement drive and to get the dream job, written test is always a nightmare. Practicing problems to solve within the given time, trying to code, unorganized hours of learning through different books and YouTube videos and finally ending up doing up NOTHING! We have come up with a unique solution of byte-sized micro learning (each video is under 5 minutes!) followed by intensive practice and analysis and hence you will able to complete your entire campus placement preparation with ease.

Connect Exams

We strongly believe that maximum outcome of any teaching can be attained only when there is 20% teaching and 80% of practice what is learnt.

With this Pareto’s Principle, we have framed CONNECT exams, a robust test engine to analyze the student on the following:

 Understanding of the problem    Time spent on each question
 Speed Vs Accuracy   Feedback on weak areas
 Suggestions to improvise
