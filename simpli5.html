It is one of our flagship training products. Apart from imparting technical training under the employability skills training vertical, we strongly felt the need for certain soft skills to be imparted for our students to make them actually industry-ready and classified those soft skills under 5 pillars collectively named as “Simpli5”

Innovation

Emphasizes the importance of staying creative and innovating with what they learn in their academics

Presentation Skills

A high-impact program to rule-out stage fear and to ace the art of public speaking

Failure Handling

A need-of-the-hour session for students to face challenges, failures, rejections not only along their academic journey but all through their life

Data Aptitude Test (DAT)

To test their problem-solving ability on 8 different parameters

Team Building

To develop inter-personal skills and to inculcate the habit of working as a team
