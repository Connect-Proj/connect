Simpli5

It is one of our flagship training products. Apart from imparting technical training under the employability skills’ training vertical, we strongly felt the need for certain soft skills to be imparted for our students to make them actually industry-ready and classified those soft skills under 5 pillars collectively named as “Simpli5”
☝ Innovation - emphasizes the importance of staying creative and innovating with what they learn in their academics
☝ Powerful Presentation Skills – a high-impact program to rule-out stage fear and to ace the art of public speaking
☝ Failure Handling – a need-of-the-hour session for students to face challenges, failures, rejections not only along their academic journey but all through their life
☝ Differential Aptitude Test (DAT) – to test their problem-solving ability on 8 different parameters.
☝ Team Building – to develop inter-personal skills and to inculcate the habit of working as a team.
Injecting these 5 pillars into our core training with lot of activities and experiential learning, our students are coming up with solutions for every problem they face and hence we are able to produce not just intellectual graduates but good human beings with value systems to make this world a better place to live.
